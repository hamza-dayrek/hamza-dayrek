## 👋 About Me

I'm **Hamza Dayrek**, a full-stack developer based in **Morocco** who enjoys building products from idea to deployment.

I work across the entire stack—from designing database architectures and building APIs to creating modern, responsive user interfaces.

Currently, I'm developing **PT35**, a multi-warehouse stock management system as my final-year project, focused on inventory management, warehouse operations, and business workflows.

During my internship at the **Commune de Mohammedia**, I built **MohammediaHub**, a civic platform designed to improve digital services and citizen engagement. That experience gave me valuable insight into building software that solves real-world problems.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually exploring SaaS ideas, validating product concepts, or turning random thoughts into working prototypes.
